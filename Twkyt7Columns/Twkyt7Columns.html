The science that deals with the theory and methods of processing information in digital computers, the design of computer hardware and software, and the applications of computers.

Calculations for left column width.

Width: 225px
Padding: 40px (20+20)
Margin: 40px (20+20)
Border: 20px (10+10)
--------------------------
Physical Width: 325px
Calculations for right column width.

Width: 187px
Padding: 40px (20+20)
Margin: 40px (20+20)
Border: 20px (10+10)
--------------------------
Physical Width: 287px

The difference between physical width and and content width is that physical width is the content width plus the margins, borders, and padding. The content width is simply the width of the items within the set.
